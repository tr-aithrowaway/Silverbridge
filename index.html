05 FAQ

Common questions.

How soon can you come out?

Usually within 1–2 business days for the Fresno/Clovis area. Same-day is often possible for emergencies — text me and ask.

Do you charge a diagnostic fee?

No. I give you a flat quote based on the issue you describe before scheduling. If something turns out to be more complex than expected, I'll quote the new scope before doing the work — never a surprise on the invoice.

What's your warranty?

14 days on labor for the specific issue we addressed. If the same problem comes back, I come back at no charge.

Can you help over the phone or remotely?

Yes — quick questions are free. For anything that requires more than 10 minutes, I use a secure remote support session billed at the hourly rate.

Do you work with businesses?

Absolutely — small offices, shops, and home-based businesses are a big part of what I do. Recurring monthly support arrangements available.

What payment methods do you take?

Cash, check, Zelle, Venmo, and major credit cards. Invoice on completion of work.
